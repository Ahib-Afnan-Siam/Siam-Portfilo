 WEAKNESSES (ranked by impact)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1. NO QUANTIFIED METRICS / IMPACT anywhere. Biggest gap. Every project and job
    bullet says WHAT was built, never the RESULT. Research: "no metrics ->
    recruiters assume no testing"; "76% of hiring managers prefer portfolios
    with detailed outcomes." Add latency, %, cost saved, users, accuracy.
    Frame with STAR (Situation-Task-Action-Result), lead with the Result.
 2. STOCK UNSPLASH IMAGES on every project. Generic AI photos read as
    "template" and hide your real work. Replace with real screenshots / GIFs.
 3. SELF-RATED SKILL % BARS (Python 95%, etc.) are a known cliche -> "95% of
    what?". Use proficiency tiers (Expert / Proficient / Familiar) or drop them.
 4. PADDED "+" STATS ("2+ years, 2+ papers, 5+ projects"). State exact numbers.
 5. NO CASE-STUDY DEPTH. No architecture diagrams or problem->approach->result
    write-ups. Make linked GitHub READMEs carry that weight.
 6. SEO was essentially absent (no metadataBase, canonical, OG image, structured
    data, robots, sitemap). FIXED in this session.

 Minor: text-justify creates uneven whitespace "rivers" (left-align reads
 better); custom cursor (cursor:none) hurt usability (addressed this session);
